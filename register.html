APPLICANT TYPE

FRESHMAN. A high school graduate who enrolls in a degree program for the first time.

TRANSFEREE. An applicant who enrolls in a degree program after having attended another college/university.

SECOND DEGREE. An applicant who seeks admission to another degree program as a second degree.

GRADUATE SCHOOL APPLICANT. An individual who seeks admission to a graduate program, such as Master’s or Doctoral Program.

FOREIGN APPLICANT. A non-resident/resident alien who comes to the Philippines and seeks admission as foreign applicant.

Reminders:
Foreigners who are applying for Graduate School programs should apply as Graduate School Applicants, while Foreigners who applied for undergraduate degree programs should apply as Foreign Applicants.

FILIPINO WHO STUDIED ABROAD. A Filipino applicant who studied in a non-Philippine school abroad.

Reminders:
Filipinos who studied from Philippine Schools Overseas should apply as Freshman, while Filipinos who studied from non-Philippine Schools Overseas should apply as Filipino who studied abroad.
